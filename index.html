Passive Drain Mode ?
This controls what the graph passively drains over time without you logging anything.

Basal BMR: Only resting metabolism drains passively. Log every swim/gym session manually — they'll show as visible dips on the graph.

BMR + Steps + NEAT: Resting metabolism + daily life activity + step goal drain passively. Still log formal exercise manually.

Full TDEE: Everything is baked in — daily life, steps, and your typical exercise load. Don't log workouts separately or you'll double-count.
